Drop your Instagram messages export and get a narrated read of your DMs — who you talk to, who you just send reels to, and what it all says about you. Everything is parsed and computed in your browser. Your messages never leave your device.

How to get your Instagram export (do this first)
  1. Instagram app → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  2. Choose Some of your information → select Messages only.
  3. Set format to JSON, date range All time, quality Low (keeps it small — we ignore media).
  4. Submit. Instagram emails a download link in minutes to a couple of days.
  5. Download the ZIP and drop it below — no need to unzip it.
